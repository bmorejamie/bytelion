Annapolis Entrepreneurs Project

Bytelion wanted to find a way to give back and the Annapolis Entrepreneurs Project was the perfect way. In an attempt to organize entrepreneurs from around the area, Bytelion built an amazing WordPress site that allows entrepreneurs to display personal profiles of themselves as well as tell others about their startup or entrepreneurial project they are working on. The site supports the new meetup group, The Annapolis Startup Meetup, which plans to serve as a catalyst behind building an entrepreneurial community and opening the lines of collaboration and innovation amongst the silos of entrepreneurs around the area.
